## Getting to Cannes

### By Plane

**Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (NCE)** — 27 km, 30-40 min

- Train Nice→Cannes: ~30 min, €7-9
- Airport bus: ~45 min, €25 one-way
- Taxi/Uber: €70-100
- Car rental: ~€50/day

Other airports: Toulon-Hyères (1.5 hrs), Milan Malpensa (4 hrs)

### By Train (TGV)

- Paris→Cannes: ~5 hours, €60-150
- Lyon→Cannes: ~4 hours, €40-100
- Milan→Cannes: ~5 hours via Nice, €50-120
- Book via SNCF Connect app or Trainline

### Driving

- Nice→Cannes: 30 min (~27 km)
- Tolls: €10-20
- Parking: ~€20/day (underground garages)

## Local Transportation

- **Walking**: Most attractions within 15-20 min walk
- **Buses**: Palm Bus, €1.50/ride or €5/day pass (Palm Bus App)
- **Regional trains**: TER to Nice (€7-9), Monaco (€12)
- **Taxis**: Starting €7, ~€2.50/km
- **Rideshare**: Uber/Bolt, €10-20 within city
- **Bikes**: €15-35/day rental

## Food & Dining

### Must-Try Local Dishes

- **Socca**: chickpea flour pancake (street food classic)
- **Bouillabaisse**: Provençal fish stew
- **Pissaladière**: onion & anchovy tart
- **Beignets de Fleurs de Courgettes**: fried zucchini flowers

### Restaurants

- **Budget**: Bar du Marin, Aux Bons Enfants, La Piastra
- **Mid-range**: Le Bistrot Gourmand, L'Affable, La Brouette de Grand-Mère
- **Fine dining**: La Palme d'Or (2 Michelin stars), Le Park 45, La Table du Chef Bruno Oger
- **Bars**: Le Bar à Vin, Morrison's Lounge, Carlton Bar

## Practical Info

### Emergency Numbers

- General emergency: 112 (EU standard)
- Medical: 15
- Police: 17
- Hospital: Centre Hospitalier de Cannes
- Tap water is safe to drink

### Useful French Phrases

- "Help!" → Au secours !
- "I need a doctor" → J'ai besoin d'un médecin
- "The bill, please" → L'addition, s'il vous plaît

### Useful Apps

- SNCF Connect / Trainline (trains)
- Palm Bus App (local buses)
- Uber / Bolt (rideshare)
- Google Maps

### Safety Tips

- Watch for pickpockets in crowds
- Beach flags: Green = safe, Yellow = caution, Red = dangerous
- Use sunscreen — the Riviera is sunny
- Validate train tickets before boarding

## Sightseeing

- **La Croisette**: luxury boulevard along the Mediterranean
- **Le Suquet (Old Town)**: cobblestone streets, Église Notre-Dame d'Espérance, panoramic views
- **Marché Forville**: indoor market, best visited mornings
- **Îles de Lérins**: ferry 15 min (€15-20), Sainte-Marguerite & Saint-Honorat islands
